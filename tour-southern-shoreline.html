Tour Overview

Discover Sri Lanka's stunning southern coastline in this relaxed 4-day beach-focused itinerary. From the UNESCO-listed Galle Fort to the whale-watching paradise of Mirissa, this tour combines colonial history, marine adventures, and pristine beach relaxation.

Perfect for those seeking a mix of cultural exploration and beach leisure, with optional adventure activities like whale watching and snorkeling available throughout the journey.
