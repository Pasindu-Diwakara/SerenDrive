Suzuki Wagon R

Category: Economy Hatchback

  • Passengers: up to 3
  • Luggage: 2 medium bags
  • Fuel: Petrol, excellent city mileage (up to 18 km/l)
  • Best for airport runs, city visits, short transfers
  • Key features: Compact parking, reliable engine, low price point

This is our most budget-friendly vehicle. We recommend it when you need basic transportation with essential comfort and great fuel economy.

Honda Shuttle

Category: Practical Wagon

  • Passengers: up to 4
  • Luggage: 3 medium bags
  • Fuel: Petrol, smooth city to highway performance
  • Best for small groups, airport + hotel shuttles, day circuits
  • Key features: Spacious rear, comfortable rear seat, stable ride

Honda Shuttle balances economy and practicality with a little extra room for luggage and passengers, ideal for compact groups who enjoy smooth city handling.

Toyota KDH (Flat Roof)

Category: Standard Minivan

  • Passengers: up to 6
  • Luggage: 5 medium bags
  • Fuel: Diesel, good efficiency for longer transfers
  • Best for families, multi-day itineraries, airport transfers with cargo
  • Key features: Easy curb access, moderate cabin height, flexible seating

This is the standard people-mover for groups who need affordable space and dependable reliability without high roof overhead charges.

Toyota KDH (High Roof)

Category: Premium Minivan

  • Passengers: up to 9
  • Luggage: 8 medium bags
  • Fuel: Diesel, best for long-route comfort
  • Best for larger groups, long hill-country journeys, comfortable load-in/out
  • Key features: Tall cabin, walk-in comfort, more overhead space

Perfect for multi-stop tours where people need to stretch, stand, and enjoy more legroom on lengthy travel days.

Toyota Prius
