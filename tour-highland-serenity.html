Tour Overview

Discover Sri Lanka's breathtaking highlands and wildlife in this comprehensive 7-day journey. From the misty peaks of Nuwara Eliya to the wildlife-rich plains of Yala, this tour combines cultural exploration with natural beauty and adventure.

Experience the romance of a scenic train journey, wander through endless tea plantations, and embark on an unforgettable safari to spot leopards and elephants in their natural habitat.

Day by Day

Detailed Itinerary

1

Day 1: Colombo to Kandy

Cultural Capital Arrival

Activities

  • • Airport pickup and transfer to Kandy
  • • Visit the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic
  • • Explore the temple complex and gardens
  • • Visit the nearby Kandy Lake
  • • Optional: Traditional Kandyan dance performance

Accommodation

4-5 star hotel in Kandy

Meals

Breakfast (hotel), Lunch & Dinner (local restaurants)

2

Day 2: Scenic Train to Ella

Mountain Journey

Activities

  • • Transfer to Kandy railway station
  • • Scenic train journey to Ella (approximately 6-7 hours)
  • • Enjoy panoramic views of mountains and tea estates
  • • Arrive in Ella and check into hotel
  • • Evening walk around Ella town

Accommodation

4-5 star hotel in Ella

Meals

Breakfast (hotel), Lunch (train/picnic), Dinner (local restaurant)

3

Day 3: Ella Exploration

Hill Country Adventures

Activities

  • • Visit Little Adam's Peak for sunrise/morning hike
  • • Explore Ella Rock and Nine Arches Bridge
  • • Visit local tea factories and plantations
  • • Optional: Ravana Ella Waterfall visit
  • • Relaxed afternoon in the cool mountain air

Accommodation

4-5 star hotel in Ella

Meals

Breakfast (hotel), Lunch & Dinner (local restaurants)

4

Day 4: Nuwara Eliya

Tea Country Discovery

Activities

  • • Transfer to Nuwara Eliya (approximately 3 hours)
  • • Visit a working tea plantation and factory
  • • Explore the Victoria Park and botanical gardens
  • • Visit the famous Gregory Lake
  • • Optional: Visit a local market for fresh produce

Accommodation

4-5 star hotel in Nuwara Eliya

Meals

Breakfast (hotel), Lunch & Dinner (local restaurants)

5

Day 5: Transfer to Yala

Journey to the Wild

Activities

  • • Transfer from Nuwara Eliya to Yala (approximately 5-6 hours)
  • • Scenic drive through changing landscapes
  • • Arrive in Yala and check into safari lodge
  • • Evening relaxation and safari briefing

Accommodation

4-5 star safari lodge in Yala

Meals

Breakfast (hotel), Lunch (en route), Dinner (lodge)

6

Day 6: Yala Safari

Wildlife Adventure

Activities

  • • Early morning safari in Yala National Park
  • • Search for leopards, elephants, and other wildlife
  • • Visit ancient Sithulpahuwa rock fortress
  • • Afternoon at leisure or optional second safari
  • • Evening wildlife observation

Accommodation

4-5 star safari lodge in Yala

Meals

Breakfast (lodge), Lunch & Dinner (lodge)

Day 7: Yala to Galle

Southern Coast & Departure

Activities
